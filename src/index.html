Welcome to ApexTimeZones

A world clock on a real 3D Earth, living in your system tray. Start by telling it where home is and pinning a few cities — they become live clocks and glowing pins on the globe.

Sports, in your time

The Sports tab maps whole seasons onto the globe — F1, the World Cup, UFC, surfing, and more. Every session shows the venue time and your time.

Star ★ a series to follow it: its next sessions appear in the Up Next strip above the globe and in your tray. Tap 🔔 on any session to get a reminder before it starts.

Convert anything, from anywhere

The Convert tab turns any event time into your time and every pinned city's — copy it, or save a reminder.

The app stays in your tray. Press Ctrl+Alt+T anywhere to show or hide it. Replay this tour anytime with the ? button.
